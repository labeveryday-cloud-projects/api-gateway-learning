About Us

Founded in 2020 by Darius Johnson and Tanya Williams, Ohio Optimal Performance LLC is Columbus's premier fitness center dedicated to empowering the Black community. With over 20 years of combined experience in fitness and nutrition, our team is committed to helping you achieve your health and wellness goals.

Our Services

Personalized Fitness Training

One-on-one sessions tailored to your fitness level and goals. Small group classes for a motivating experience. Specialized programs: weight loss, muscle gain, athletic performance.

Nutrition Coaching

Customized meal plans that respect cultural food preferences. Cooking classes and nutritional workshops to promote healthy living.
